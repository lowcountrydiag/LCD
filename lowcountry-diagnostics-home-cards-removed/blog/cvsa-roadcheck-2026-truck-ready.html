Why this matters

Annual inspection blitz weeks create pressure for fleets. A pre-check can reduce avoidable downtime and violations. For fleets and owner-operators, the goal is not just passing an inspection or clearing a fault. The goal is keeping the truck productive, safe, and ready for work.

What to check

  • Look for obvious defects before the truck leaves the yard.
  • Document maintenance and repair decisions clearly.
  • Do not ignore warning lights, leaks, electrical issues, brake concerns, or recurring faults.
  • Use diagnostics to confirm the root cause instead of replacing parts blindly.
